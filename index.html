Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you cater?+
We cater all types of events — Weddings & Celebrations, Religious & Cultural Events, Corporate & Professional Gatherings, Social & Entertainment Events, Public & Community Functions, and Digital & Modern Events. Our team handles everything from intimate private gatherings to large-scale national events.
Do you provide vegetarian and Jain food options?+
Yes. We are 100% pure vegetarian. We also offer complete Jain menus with no onion, no garlic, and no root vegetables — prepared separately in dedicated areas to maintain purity. Special dietary requirements can also be accommodated when informed in advance.
Can we customize the menu for our event?+
Absolutely. Our menus are fully customizable. You can choose dishes from our 700+ item catalogue, combine cuisines, select live counters, or create an entirely bespoke menu. Our team will help you design the perfect spread based on your guest count, event type, and preferences.
Do you provide live food counters?+
Yes! We offer 15 speciality live counters including Italian Pasta, Chinese Wok, Mexican, Thai, Chaat, Dosa, Live Jalebi, Pizza, BBQ, and Dessert stations. Live counters create an interactive, entertaining dining experience that guests absolutely love. Available with Silver, Gold, Diamond, and Platinum packages.
What is the most popular menu for weddings?+
Wedding clients typically choose a combination of traditional North Indian or Gujarati dishes, 2–3 live food counters (Italian, Chinese, and Chaat are fan favourites), and an elaborate dessert station. The Gold and Diamond packages are most popular for weddings, giving a perfect balance of variety and value.
How far in advance should we book?+
We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance to secure your date. For peak wedding season (October–February) and popular wedding dates, we strongly advise booking 2–3 months ahead. Once your date is confirmed with us, it is reserved exclusively for your event.
Do you provide tasting before booking?+
Yes! We offer free food tasting sessions for weddings and large events. You can sample dishes from our 700+ item menu and evaluate quality before making any commitment. Tasting sessions are by prior appointment — call us at +91 98250 95714 to schedule yours.
How can we book your catering service?+
You can book in three easy ways: (1) Fill out the booking inquiry form on this page, (2) Call us directly at +91 98250 95714 or +91 98980 77416, or (3) Send an inquiry via WhatsApp. Our team will contact you within 24 hours to discuss event details, menu preferences, and finalize your booking.
What happens after I send a booking request?+
Our team reviews your request and contacts you within 24 hours. We then schedule a call or meeting to discuss your event requirements, guest count, menu preferences, and budget. Based on this, we prepare a customized menu and service plan tailored to your event — no generic proposals.
Can you handle last-minute event bookings?+
If the date is available, we can arrange catering on short notice. However, earlier booking guarantees better preparation, full menu flexibility, and our best team allocation. For urgent bookings, call us directly for the fastest response.
What is included in your catering service?+
Our standard catering service includes: Food preparation with fresh ingredients, Complete buffet setup, Professional serving staff, Basic food presentation and display, and Live counter setup (based on package). Additional services like special décor elements, extra staff, or extended service hours can be added based on your event needs.
Do you provide catering outside the city?+
Yes! We offer PAN INDIA service. We have catered events across Gujarat, Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Delhi, and many more states. Travel and logistics arrangements may apply depending on distance. Contact us to discuss your out-of-city event — we travel wherever your celebration is.
How many guests can you serve?+
We comfortably cater events ranging from 50 to 5000+ guests. With proper planning and staff allocation, we have successfully managed events with 5000+ guests. Our team scales operations based on your requirements to ensure consistent quality regardless of event size.
What happens if guest count changes?+
We can adjust the food quantity if the guest count changes, provided we are informed at least 48 hours before the event. We understand that guest lists change — our flexible approach ensures you never run short or waste excess food.
Can you manage very large guest events smoothly?+
Absolutely. With our 11+ years of experience and 1900+ events completed last decade, large-scale events are our specialty. We cook in controlled batches, deploy trained chefs and supervisors on-site, and have robust logistics in place to maintain food quality and service consistency across thousands of guests.
Do you provide staff and serving arrangements?+
Yes. Our complete catering service includes professional chefs, trained serving staff, and full buffet setup management. We handle everything from food station arrangement to guest-facing service — so you can relax and enjoy your own event.
What safety and hygiene standards do you follow?+
We maintain strict food hygiene standards throughout. This includes using fresh ingredients sourced daily, professional kitchen equipment, sanitary food handling practices, and regular quality checks. All our chefs follow food safety protocols to ensure every dish served is safe, fresh, and delicious.
Why do most clients choose RTC for their events?+
Clients choose RTC for consistent food quality, reliable service staff, and flexible menu customization. Our focus is not just food quantity but taste, presentation, and the overall guest experience. With 2100+ five-star reviews and 98% client satisfaction, most of our bookings come from repeat customers and warm referrals.
Can guests with dietary restrictions be accommodated?+
Yes. We accommodate all dietary requirements including Jain (no onion, garlic, root vegetables), low-spice for elderly guests, nut-free options, and other special dietary needs. Simply inform us in advance and we will prepare dedicated options for those guests — handled with complete care and separation.
